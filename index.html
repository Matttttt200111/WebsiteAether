About the Studio

Passion Meets Precision

With over a decade on the touchlines, in the arenas, and behind the studio lights, Aether is built around one obsession: capturing the raw, unscripted energy of sport at its finest.

Based in Maribor and working across Europe, I cover everything from grassroots fixtures and professional leagues to studio athlete portraits and black-tie sports galas.

Every commission — regardless of scale — receives the same relentless attention to detail and creative ambition.
